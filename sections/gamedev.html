Timber

Timber is a game about a tribe of characters rebuilding their home in the woods.
Players can only use one button when interacting with the game. Player can control time and use it to choose which resource to prioritize. Watch your tribe evolve into the next level.

Submission to the One Button Game Jam 2020.
