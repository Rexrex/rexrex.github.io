ABOUT ME

My name is Manuel Guimarães and I'm a Portuguese PhD Student at Instituto Superior Técnico and a researcher at GAIPS, INESC-ID in Lisbon, Portugal.

Since I started as an Artificial Intelligence Researcher since 2016 I have delved into many different fields such as Human Computer Interaction, Intelligent Virtual Agents, Game Development, Mixed Reality and Machine Learning.
At the moment I am focused on finishing my Doctoral Thesis, which is about creating a framework that can facilitate the task of creating believable Social Intelligent Agents through Data Driven Approach. My research interests are in artificial intelligence, virtual agents, affective computing, social interaction and their application in games.
I am one of the founders and coordinators of Game Dev Técnico: a student’s group focused “on providind students at our university with the resources and mentorship required to develop their own games in an extracurricular and not for profit way.”
